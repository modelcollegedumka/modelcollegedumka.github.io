Model College Dumka was established in 2022 and is located in Bijaypur, approximately 10 km from Sido Kanhu Murmu University and 5 km from the Dumka railway station. The college provides undergraduate education in a variety of fields, including B.A., B.Sc., B.Com, and B.CA. Admissions to these programs are conducted directly in accordance with the guidelines set by the Government of Jharkhand and Sido Kanhu Murmu University.
Model College Dumka operates under the jurisdiction of Sido Kanhu Murmu University (SKMU), a prominent public university in the Santhal Parganas region of Jharkhand. For more information about the university and its affiliations, visit the official website: skmu.ac.in.
